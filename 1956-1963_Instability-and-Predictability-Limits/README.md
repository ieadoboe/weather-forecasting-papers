## Chaos and Predictability Limits (1963)

- **Phillips, N.A., 1956** _"The general circulation of the atmosphere: A numerical experiment,"_ Quarterly Journal of the Royal Meteorological Society
  - Phillips' 1956 GCM experiment
  - First successful general circulation simulation demonstrating baroclinic instability

- **Lorenz, E.N. (1963).** _"Deterministic nonperiodic flow."_ Journal of the Atmospheric Sciences, 20, 130-141.
  - Discovered sensitive dependence on initial conditions (butterfly effect); established fundamental limits on predictability
