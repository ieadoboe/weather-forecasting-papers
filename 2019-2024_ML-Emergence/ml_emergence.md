## Machine Learning Emergence (2019-2024)

* **Weyn, J.A., Durran, D.R., & Caruana, R. (2019).** *"Can machines learn to predict weather? Using deep learning to predict gridded 500-hPa geopotential height from historical weather data."* Journal of Advances in Modeling Earth Systems, 11, 2680-2693.

    * Early demonstration of CNNs for weather forecasting; competitive with simple baselines

* **Rasp, S., et al. (2020).** *"WeatherBench: A benchmark data set for data-driven weather forecasting."* Journal of Advances in Modeling Earth Systems, 12, e2020MS002203.

    * Established standardized benchmark for comparing ML weather models

* **Keisler, R. (2022).** *"Forecasting global weather with graph neural networks."* arXiv preprint arXiv:2202.07575.

    * Introduced GNNs for global weather forecasting

* **Lam, R., et al. (2023).** *"Learning skillful medium-range global weather forecasting."* Science, 382, 1416-1421.

    * GraphCast paper: First ML model matching operational NWP skill (ECMWF HRES) for medium-range forecasts

* **Bi, K., et al. (2023).** *"Accurate medium-range global weather forecasting with 3D neural networks."* Nature, 619, 533-538.

    * Pangu-Weather paper: 3D Earth-specific architectures for weather forecasting; competitive with GraphCast

* **Pathak, J., et al. (2024).** *"Probabilistic weather forecasting with machine learning."* Nature, 632, 8252.

    * GenCast paper: First ML probabilistic ensemble forecast exceeding ECMWF ENS
