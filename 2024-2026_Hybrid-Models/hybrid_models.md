## Hybrid Models: NeuralGCMs (2024)

* **Kochkov, D., Yuval, J., Langmore, I., et al. (2024).** *"Neural general circulation models for weather and climate."* Nature, 632, 1060-1066.

    * Culmination - hybrid model combining differentiable dynamical core with ML parameterizations; competitive for both weather (1-15 days) AND climate (decades)

* **Yuval, J., Kochkov, D., et al. (2024).** *"Neural general circulation models for modeling precipitation."* Science Advances (in press, described in Google Research blog).

    * Extension of NeuralGCM trained directly on satellite precipitation observations; improved small-scale physics representation
